Step 1 of 3

Notice of Privacy Practices (HIPAA)

Please read how we protect and use your health information.

How your information is used

MEC Eye Specialists uses your protected health information (PHI) to provide treatment, obtain payment for services, and operate our practice. We may share information with other providers involved in your care, such as your primary care physician.

Your rights

You have the right to inspect and request a copy of your records, request corrections, request restrictions on certain uses, and receive a list of disclosures. You may request confidential communications through a specific channel.

Our commitment

We are required by law to maintain the privacy of your PHI and to notify you in the event of a breach. This notice applies to all records of your care generated by our practice.

Step 2 of 3

Consent to treat & financial policy

Two acknowledgments before we begin your care.

Consent for treatment

I voluntarily consent to eye examinations, diagnostic testing, and medical or surgical treatment as deemed necessary by the physicians of MEC Eye Specialists. I understand that no guarantee has been made regarding the outcome of treatment, and that I may ask questions or decline any procedure.

Financial responsibility

I understand that I am financially responsible for any charges not covered by my insurance, including copays, deductibles, and non-covered services. I authorize my insurance benefits to be paid directly to MEC Eye Specialists.
