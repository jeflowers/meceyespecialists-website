Meet the Team

The specialists caring for your sight

Since 2001, our physicians and optometrists have cared for communities from Downtown Los Angeles to Long Beach — combining deep expertise with genuine, patient-first attention.

Founder

Sandeep Khanna, M.D.

Vitreo-Retinal Surgery · Founder

Dr. Khanna founded Mercy Eye Care Medical Group (MEC) in 2001 to provide eye care services in the Mid-Cities region of Los Angeles. MEC now comprises multiple offices from Downtown Los Angeles to Long Beach. He directed the Vitreo-Retinal Services at the MLK Jr. Multi-Service Ambulatory Care Center (MACC), where he earned the “Doctor of the Year” award in 2013. His clinical interests include diabetic retinopathy, age-related macular degeneration, and the management of complex retinal detachments.

Newest Member

Charles W. Flowers Jr., M.D.

Cornea & Refractive Surgery · LASIK

The newest member of our team, Dr. Flowers has over 20 years of experience performing LASIK eye surgery and is a nationally recognized leader in the field. He is committed to providing the highest quality and most advanced visual corrective procedures, and is regarded by both patients and peers for his surgical skill and the warm, caring way in which he relates to his patients.

His involvement in Laser Vision Correction began in 1993 with a coveted fellowship at USC's Doheny Eye Institute, where he conducted basic research and authored several book chapters and articles on the subject. He went on to join the faculties of UCLA's Jules Stein Eye Institute and the Charles R. Drew University Department of Ophthalmology, dividing his time between teaching, research, and clinical practice — securing over one million dollars in research grant funding and rising to Associate Professor.

He has been recognized repeatedly as one of the nation's top laser eye surgeons by the Consumers' Research Council of America and as a top LASIK surgeon by San Diego Magazine, and has served as a medical consultant for the FOX 6 XETV Morning Show. Dr. Flowers has been providing corneal sub-specialty services to Guam since 2010.
